Basis Simulator

Simulation Software for Robots.

Basis is a lightweight robot simulator with realistic physics. Collect data for machine learning and AI experiments, simulate automation tasks, and use our level builder to create your own custom environments!

  • Simulate, Develop, and Research with Ease.

    No lengthy multi-day installs. Just download and run. Share your work easily with others through common file formats.

  • Realistic Physics.

    Use the powerful industry-tested Bullet physics engine to see how robots can interact with the scenes and objects you setup.

  • Generate and Record Data, Script with Python.

    Store and record every sensor reading and action taken by the robot. Ideal for machine learning and AI. Intuitive python scripting to simulate and do research on your robots. Integrates seamlessly with machine learning and computer vision libraries.
