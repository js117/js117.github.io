About Us: Company Background

Empirical Automation was founded by James Schuback in February, 2016. Inspired by recent robot AI demos from US universities, the venture started as an exploration into what robotics hardware would be affordable for trying to replicate these experiments. However, we quickly found a large gap in the market between 50K+ industrial robots, and small impractical toys. Through diligent prototyping and user pilot trials, the venture outgrew the apartment living room and gained the support of internationally renowned startup programs and incubators.

Founder Profile

James Schuback is a Canadian engineer and serial entrepreneur with a first principles approach to life. He is currently Founder and CEO of Empirical Automation. James has a wide range of engineering and managerial experience in fields such as wearable technology, semiconductors, robotics, software development, machine learning, and hardware design. James graduated with honors from Engineering Science (major in Electrical and Computer Engineering), Canada’s most prestigious engineering program. He is a self-taught roboticist and artificial intelligence developer. James has been selected twice to participate in Next Canada entrepreneurship programs, the country’s most reputable and selective organization for young entrepreneurs. Previously the founder of a wearable technology company, James developed and sold novel intellectual property to a larger competitor. At Empirical he is responsible for product design, business development, and strategic vision.
