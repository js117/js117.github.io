Features

INDUSTRIAL QUALITY

A robot arm must incorporate high quality engineering design to create economic value for end users. Our robots are precise, durable, fast, collaborative, and modular. With sub-millimeter repeatability, Prometheus can tackle high end manufacturing tasks. But our real mission is to bring this quality to small and medium manufacturers, opening new possibilities not previously imagined.

USER INTERFACE

We have created the most intuitive robot automation software yet. The Prometheus Tablet App lets users drag, drop, point, and click, to easily set up new automated welding tasks. Define path parameters, unit repeat timings, and arc parameters without any training necessary.

FLEXIBLE FINANCING

Our mission is to make high quality robotics automation more accessible than ever before. While we've been able to create an incredibly capable and affordable platform, our efforts don't stop there. We have various rental programs, as well as flexible leasing options. Please contact us for more information.

Modularity

Different jobs call for different tools. The Prometheus platform allows users to switch welding systems, and is designed to be flexible to different end user applications. Contact us for more details.

FAQ

Below are answers to the most frequently asked questions we have received.

Q: What is the beta program trial?
A: The beta program is an opportunity for businesses to get a faster, low cost version of the Prometheus welding robot in exchange for providing concept and prototype feedback as we refine and head into production. Only available in North America. Beta users will pay approximately 40% of the retail price, take part in a 3-6 month trial using the robot, and have this cost go towards their reserved finished product.

Q: What is the deadline to apply to the beta program trial?
A: Beta trials are on-going, with applications open until June, 2017.

Q: Do I have to be affiliated with a business or can I apply as an individual/hobbyists?
A: Absolutely you can! However, due to the volume of applicants we will have to carefully select where we deploy our early version hardware.

Q: How does Pre-Ordering work?
A: Pre-Orders are for the purpose of locking in your commitment for our official launch. Think of it as saving your spot in line for our first production run. We require a 10% down-payment on each robot, and are currently limiting the maximum pre-order quantity to 25 robots per customer. Note that pre-orders are completely protected and refunded if products are not delivered.

Q: When will the first products be shipped?
A: September 1st, 2017. Note that we will be delivering beta versions at an on-going basis during the beta trials currently underway.

Q: Are specifications subject to change?
A: Between the beta trial and final launch, we may make minor modifications to Prometheus's rated specifications. These would include small percentage changes in various quantities as we test and refine, and thus shouldn't differ to the point where they affect usage in any significantly way. In fact we expect a few parameters to improve!

Q: How much does the robot weigh? How do I move and mount it?
A: Prometheus weighs approximately 180 lbs. Handles will be available to attach to the sides of the robot, and we've found that it is easily portable around the lab using a furniture dolly. The base of the robot is very sturdy and designed to limit workplace vibrations. Early testers have put Prometheus everywhere from tabletops, to lab benches, to mobile platforms, or just on the factory floor.

Q: Where is your company based? Can I visit?
A: Big Solve Robotics was founded by James Schuback, and currently operates in Toronto, Canada. Feel free to reach out if you're in the area - james@bigsolverobotics.com
